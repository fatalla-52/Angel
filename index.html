Discover the flavor of Thailand

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spieces. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, combine with succulent shirmp.

The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balangce of flavors - sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.

- Chef Somchai

Ingredients:

  • Lemongrass: 2 stalk
  • Thai basil: 1 cup
  • kaffir Lime leaves 3 leaves
  • shrimp 500g

Preparation:

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink
  4. Season with fish souce, lime juice, and chili paste.
  5. Garnish with Thia basil and serve hot.

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4 cup of fish souce add authentic Thai flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat
